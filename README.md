# Hi there I’m Lucas González-Biedma

I’m a **Chilean sociologist (MA, Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile)** working at the intersection of **quantitative and cualitative social research** and **policy-relevant evidence**. My work spans **youth justice and delinquency**, **political identities**, **vicarious memories of political violence**, and applied research in public institutions and consulting.

- Currently: **Department of Studies** of the **National Youth Social Reintegration Service (SNRSJ)** belonging to the Ministry of Justice and Human Rights of Chile.
- Background: Academic research projects (**FONDECYT** / **Millennium Institutes**) + consulting (**SUMMA**, **INDH**)
- Methods: **R** and **Stata** for data management, visualization, and statistical inference; qualitative analysis with **NVivo / Atlas.ti**
- Language: **Spanish (native)** and **English (fluent)**

---

## What I do
- Turn complex datasets into **clear, decision-useful insights** (cleaning → analysis → figures → reporting)
- Build **reproducible quantitative workflows** in R (and continuously improving my Git-based workflow)
- Work comfortably across **research, policy, and interdisciplinary teams**
- Produce evidence for **decision-making** and **understanding** complex social phenomena
